04/12/2014

PinPoint.zone

This is an app that I started building at the end of last year. I have not had a chance to deploy to the app or play store yet. It's an app that scrapes ads off of Kijiji and Craigslist. It gets each individual ads location, and shows the user who is selling what around them. At the same time, it allows users to create their own ads. Everytime a user creates an ad, it automitically attaches the user's location to it. Therefore if you are at a particular location, looking to buy an iphone, it will show you people around you who have an iphone for sale, and it allows you to directly message them through the app. For the desktop version, I build a real-time dashboard, that I use to run my scrapers on different webpages. This is version 1 of the app. I have not integrated the chat feature to completion yet. I am not done designing it yet either.
